WELCOME TO THE WORLD OF STREET FIGHTER !

USAGE:

-   ./my_hunter

DESCRIPTION:

-   The goal is to shoot enemies on the march and not loose your 6 lifes !
-   each time you miss your shoot, you lose 1 life.
-   The speed will grow as far as you get points.
-   Let ennemies pass the screen will take you one life.

CONTROLS:

- UpArrow and DownArrow allow you to move on the menu
- Space to select in the menu
- Escape to get back to the menu
- Press P to Pause the game and R to resume
- And the most important, left click to shoot !

Your Highscore will be stored, so try to beat it again and again
to be the most valuable player of Hadoken Simulator !

Have a good fight !
